Our Story

A Shop Built on Trust

Shri Sideshwar Agro Agency was founded by Ishwar Shitole at C.S.M. Chowk, Pimpalgaon (Rahu) with a single mission — to give farmers in Daund taluka access to genuine, certified agricultural products at honest prices.

Over the years we have grown from a small fertilizer counter to a full-service agro input shop trusted by hundreds of farming families across sugarcane, onion, wheat, and vegetable belts of Pune district.

KrushiConnect is our next step — bringing our product catalog online so every farmer can check availability, compare prices, and contact us directly from their phone without traveling to the shop.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

From a small agro counter to a digital platform — every step was for the farmer.

2013
Shop Founded

Ishwar Shitole opens Shri Sideshwar Agro Agency at C.S.M. Chowk, Pimpalgaon (Rahu).

2016
Expanded Product Range

Added insecticides, pesticides, and seeds alongside fertilizers. Became a one-stop agro shop.

2020
WhatsApp Ordering

Farmers started ordering via WhatsApp during lockdown. We adapted and made it a permanent service.

2025
KrushiConnect Launched

Full digital catalog with admin panel, farmer accounts, favorites, and inquiry system — live for all farmers.
